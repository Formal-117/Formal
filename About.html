A bit more about us.

We are a small web-development company based in Canada, and we have been building websites since 2016. We take pride in our work, hence we make sure to provide you with the best service possible. At FORMAL our main goal is to give our customers a website that will stand out and be unique. The internet has a lot of websites, so making sure your website stands out is our top priority. A visually appealing website can help you and your business stand out .

Starting a business, want an online portfolio, holding a new event? We can help you with any of those, from the point of you getting your very own website to you having all your social media organized and managed by our expert online marketers. We can help you with building an online presence, and our price ranges make sure we have something for everyone. We provide various additional services in the form of packages and at very reasonable prices. With a simple ordering process and a quick chat with us, you'll have your website designed in no time. We also provide cheap web hosting for only 2$ a month.
